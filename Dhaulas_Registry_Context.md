- The buyer has physically visited the land, verified it on-site, and the seller has vacated.

- The buyer has accepted the land in its current condition.

**Clause 3 — Title Guarantee (Page 15):**
- The sold land has clean, transferable title (Swachh Antaran Yogya Swamitva).
- If any defect in seller's ownership, title, or any other reason causes the buyer to lose possession of any part of the land, the seller is liable to compensate the buyer for the full cost including expenses from the seller's movable and immovable assets.
- Neither the seller nor their heirs/successors have the right to object.

**Clause 4 — Mutation Support (Page 15):**
- The seller will fully cooperate with the buyer for Namantaran (mutation/name transfer) proceedings.
- The buyer has the right to get the seller's name removed from revenue records and register themselves as owner.
- The seller will provide any required documents, affidavits, etc. at the buyer's expense.

**Clause 5 — Easement Rights (Page 15):**
- All easement rights pass to the buyer — including right of way (Malikanaa Rasta), drainage (Naali), air (Hawa), water (Paani), light (Roshani), and all present and future rights that the seller had as of the date of sale.

**Clause 6 — Definition of Parties (Page 17):**
